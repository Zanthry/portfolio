The Intersection of Code & Charcoal

I am a Software Engineer and a Charcoal Artist. My work seeks to explore the dichotomy between the precision of digital engineering and the raw, imperfect nature of charcoal dust.

Through this digital sanctuary, the lines of code breathe life into still strokes, bridging two distinct disciplines into a unified aesthetic experience.
